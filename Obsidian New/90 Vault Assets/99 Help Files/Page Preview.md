# Page Preview
#rwertz #plugins/core  

---
## Settings
- Require `Ctrl/Cmd`to trigger a page preview of linked notes or attachments on hover: 
	- Search, Backlinks, Outgoing links: `On`
	- Reading view: `Off`
	- Editing view: `On`
	- Tag pane: `On`
	- Files: `On`
